Yuji Itadori

Yuji Itadori is a character in Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High, where he studies to become a Jujutsu Sorcerer.

Gojo Saturo

Satoru Gojo is a fictional character in the manga and anime series Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a special-grade jujutsu sorcerer with white hair and striking blue eyes. He is also the first person in 400 years to inherit the "Six Eyes" and "Limitless" skills.
